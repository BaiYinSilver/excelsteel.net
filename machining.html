Machining Introduction

With highly versatile machining capabilities, we can provide precise components regardless of variation in design complexity. We guarantee consistent quality and low cost for your machined components.

Our machining operations include Turning, Milling, Grinding, Punching, Drilling, Bending, Swaging, Tapping and Threading, either by CNC or conventional methods. We can machine a wide variety of materials, including aluminum, brass, alloys, stainless steel and carbon steels. Our specialty is high volume production runs. We can effectively accommodate large quantity orders, while maintaining excellent quality and rapidity to market.
