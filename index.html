Welcome to Excelsteel Manufacturing Ltd (ESM)

Who is Excelsteel Manufacturing Ltd (ESM)?

ESM is a global, vertically integrated manufacturer/supplier of metal components and assemblies. As a modern, progressive, and forward-looking company, ESM continues to evolve and diversify as new technologies and opportunities emerge in and around the markets in which we work. ESM shares clients’ concern in quality, service, and price.

With factories in China, ESM is able to offer fairly low prices for products made from steel, and other materials.

All ESM products are customised. The capabilities fully cover steel casting, steel forging, metal stamping, full machining and fabrication.

No matter whether you are in automotive, agricultural, oil and gas, construction industries or any other industrial sectors, when you need quality and competitively priced metal products in the market, we can work together, and you will be satisfied with the outcome.

ESM not only sells its strong and versatile capabilities in metal manufacturing, but also ensures worry free services from production to shipment.

Building long- term relationships is a key to success. ESM looks forward to growing alongside its clients.
