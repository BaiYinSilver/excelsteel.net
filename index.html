Welcome to Excelsteel Manufacturing (ESM)

Excelsteel Manufacturing is a global, vertically integrated manufacturer/supplier of metal components and assemblies for the automotive, agricultural, oil and gas, construction industries. Our factories in China offer fairly low prices for products made from steel, as well as from other materials

As a modern, progressive, and forward-looking company, ESM continues to evolve and diversify as new technologies and opportunities emerge in and around the markets in which we work.

With over 20 years of experience, ESM has provided quality products for the domestic and international market. In a highly competitive market environment, ESM's experience enables us to provide high quality products, excellent service, and quick and safe delivery to satisfy customers' need through casting, forging, fabrication, and machining processes involved.
